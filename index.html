Notes

Beck & B&C are complementary. B&C gives the abstract Hilbert-space view (prox = resolvent, monotone operators); Beck gives explicit prox computations + FISTA in ℝⁿ. Read both prox chapters — they do different jobs.

Weeks 5–6 are the tightest. R&W Ch. 7 (epi-convergence) genuinely requires Ch. 4 (set convergence), and §12.D ties it back to the prox. Skip proofs on a first reading if you fall behind.

Strict priority: O1 + O2 first; pure O3/O4 sits at the back.
